Me and Earl and the Dying Girl

Me and Earl and the Dying Girl is a heartfelt coming-of-age film that follows Greg, a high school senior who prefers to stay under the radar and avoid emotional connections. His world changes when his mother forces him to spend time with Rachel, a classmate diagnosed with leukemia. As they form an unlikely friendship, Greg and his quirky friend Earl create low-budget parody films together to cheer up Rachel.
